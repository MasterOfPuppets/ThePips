The Pips

Motivation

I commute every day to work, always listening to music with my android phone and I don't like wearing a watch, so the need to know the time comes once in a while. That's why I made "The Pips" - to be notified that time is ticking.

What you can do with it

  • Set the notification interval, you can choose one of three levels (one hour, half hour or fifteen minutes.
  • You can choose to be notified only when phones are plugged in or be always notified.
  • You can use the built in pips (see wikipedia page)
  • ... or your phone usual notifications.
  • ... or you can set the pips to be different at each quarter
